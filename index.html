All household services are available in one place: video conference with an electrician or a plumber, repair works maintenance, laundry or cleaning, even gardening

RGS is an insurance company with 12.9 million customers. In 2020 it has decided to expand its services and launch startups under the brand My_Service — Dom, Med, Auto. My_Service Dom helps to deal with household issues: change or repair bathroom facilities and electricity, schedule annual control of utility systems throughout, control the repair works and order cleaning services. From 2021 to 2022 we were managing the product design, research and brand development. Dom and Auto are made within one design-system. There is a catalogue of over 100 services inside each project. Neither of the services may be announced as priority ones since each of them is popular among the users. Search button has become the central object on the page. The colour differentiation has simplified the searching and completes the navigation. In case of an urgent matter there is an online master available 24/7. It’s a specialist with knowledge and skills of an electrician, a plumber and a locksmith. He can help to deal with the problem or order a service needed. Afterwards he can also inform how to avoid malfunctions in the future. After JTBD-research we divided all the services into urgent and non-urgent. For the urgent ones we tried to make business processes more beneficial for the customer. For example, to eliminate the pipe leak using video conference with a plumber and only after proceeding with the payment. In case something important has broken down or gone out of control, a person seeks a fast solution and tries to find his problem among hundreds of services. The graphic reading speed must be high to meet that expectation. Graphics detailing aims to be realistic yet with little simplifications to avoid excessive noise within small forms where an object is perceived as an icon

All the auto services are available in one place: video conference with a car mechanic, towing service, repair maintenance, seasonal service, assistance with a car sale or purchase

RGS is an insurance company with 12.9 million customers. In 2020 it has decided to expand its services and launch startups under the brand My_Service — Auto, Dom, Med. My_Service Auto takes care of a car or a motorcycle: technical or legal consultations, repair works maintenance, evacuation, seasonal service, assistance with a car sale or purchase. From 2021 to 2022 we were managing the product design, research and brand development. Auto and Dom are made within one design-system. There is a catalogue of over 100 services inside each project. Neither of the services may be announced as priority ones since each of them is popular among the users. Search button has become the central object on the page. The colour differentiation has simplified the searching and completes the navigation. In case of an urgent matter, an auto consultant (an online specialist with a car mechanic experience) is available 24/7. He can help to deal with the problem and order a necessary service. Legal and technical consultants will provide support after accidents and car repair. After JTBD-research we divided all the services into urgent and non-urgent. For the urgent ones we tried to make business processes more beneficial for the customer. For example, first order a tow truck and then, during the waiting, deal with the service payment. In case something important has broken down or gone out of control, a person seeks a fast solution and tries to find his problem among hundreds of services. The graphic reading speed must be high to meet that expectation. Graphics detailing aims to be realistic yet with little simplifications to avoid “excessive noise” within small forms where an object is perceived as an icon.

Yandex Pacticum helps people to acquire new professions and skills with the help of technology and a modern approach and a large amount of practice.

Yandex Practicum is a large company with many directions. I was responsible for the direction of the English language. Our design team has been developing and improving the platform. Together with a team of experts, we tried to make learning English interesting and unusual. We were looking to find new, non-boring approaches to learning. The product was in search of product-market-fit, so most often we were looking for new hypotheses and did a lot of experiments. Learning a language is a difficult process for a person and it was most difficult to work with the motivation of a student to overcome the language barrier and laziness. We tried to make a clean, pleasant and emotional product. Many users noted how the product communicated with students, we spent a lot of time writing texts and motivating students to continue learning. Our product has become one of the benchmarks in English language learning and many product approaches have been copied by our competitors.

Teko is a marketplace of business applications that helps its customers to make effective sales online and offline.

In this project, I helped the business to assemble all its services into products understandable to the client and present them on the company's website. Teko works in different directions, from selling its products on marketplaces to organizing a warehouse and an offline point of sale for a cashier. We have come up with a modular system that allows the company to quickly collect and talk about its current products, as well as collect new landing pages for new and future proposals and experiments. Customers liked the style of the new website so much that they gradually began to use it in their internal interfaces and products.
